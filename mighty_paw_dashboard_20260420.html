Step 1 — set target days of supply per channel
How to read this: Enter your target Days of Supply (DOS) per channel below. WH 240 (Rawson Road 240) replenishes all 7 marketplaces. Each marketplace already has some units in its own pool (FBA, FBT, etc.) — WH 240 tops up any gap. The WH 240 surplus is what remains in WH 240 after every channel is fully topped up to its target DOS. A green surplus = buffer stock available. A "—" = WH 240 is being fair-shared.
